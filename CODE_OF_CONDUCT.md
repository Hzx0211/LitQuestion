# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

People participating in this project are expected to:

- Be direct, respectful, and technically honest
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people
- Share repro steps, logs, screenshots, and evidence when reporting problems
- Assume good intent, but keep discussions concrete and accountable

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following are not acceptable:

- Personal attacks, harassment, or intimidation
- Discriminatory language
- Repeated low-signal arguments that derail the technical discussion
- Sharing private data, secrets, or someone else's credentials

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other repository interactions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors whose behavior makes collaboration harder or unsafe.
